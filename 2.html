My Education

I received my schooling at St. Peter's School in Bharatpur. After that, I enrolled at Vibrant Academy Kota, where I completed my 11th and 12th grades and studied for the JEE. In my first attempt, I received a rank of 6891. (thanks to homesickness). After that, I dropped at Allen for a year and eventually got 223 CRL and admission to IITK for computer science. I may not appear conventional at first since I have an other perspective on life I see it as a chance to learn about the cosmos rather than squandering it in dread. Most of the time, I may be lost in my own world, appreciating nature's beauty.
